Questions Answered

How long does a typical project take?
Timelines vary by scope. A typical MVP takes 4–8 weeks. Enterprise platforms can range from 3–6 months. We always provide a detailed project timeline before kickoff.
Do you work with early-stage startups?
Absolutely. We love partnering with founders from day one. Our Starter plan is built exactly for early-stage teams who need to move fast and validate ideas efficiently.
What tech stack do you use?
We're full-stack agnostic and choose the best tool for each job. Commonly: React/Next.js, Node.js, Python (FastAPI), PostgreSQL, AWS/GCP, and state-of-the-art LLM APIs for AI products.
Who owns the IP and source code?
You do — 100%. All code, design assets, and intellectual property created during the engagement belong exclusively to you upon final payment.
Do you offer ongoing support after launch?
Yes. We offer flexible retainer packages for ongoing development, maintenance, monitoring, and feature evolution. Many clients continue working with us long after launch.
